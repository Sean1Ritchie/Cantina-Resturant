Our Story

An ode to
gathering &
good wine

Cantina was born from a simple belief: that the most profound dining experiences happen when barriers dissolve — between kitchen and table, between continents of flavour, between strangers who share a bottle.

In partnership with Le Grand Domaine, we have created an intimate space in the heart of Stellenbosch that echoes the spirit of a Basque pintxos bar — convivial, unhurried, and deeply alive with flavour.
